Your personal pocket guide to the world's urban jungles

Our mission is to take the disorientation out of travel, providing you the support needed to adopt a more authentic, rawer form of exploration.

Some of our features

Digitial DNA

Using smart algorithms, we build you a personal Digital DNA, and use this to constantly learn about you as a person, improving your time while travelling.

Mobile Ready Functionality

Soon to be available on iOS and Android, you don’t need to rely on a web platform, because your personal travel assistant will now be a mobile download.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Location Services are just some of the exciting technologies we’re using to make your experience unique.

How does it work?

KOMPAS will impact the way people travel by moving away from the existing one-dimensional digital guides, towards a fluid, handpicked collection of places that adapts to your personality over time. All of this is neatly packaged into a simple, easy-to-use application that will save you time and effort, offering that reliable guiding hand when you are out exploring the unknown.
