Your pocket guide to the world's urban jungles

Say good bye to the endless planning and getting hopelessly lost, only to end up visiting typical tourist traps and generic cafés in a city.

Our mission is to take the disorientation out of travel and provide a more authentic, and enjoyable form of city exploration.

We present you with truly unique and personalised experiences, tailored to your interests. Have fun exploring the world in a whole new way!

What makes us different?

Personalised Itineraries

Fluid and adaptable itineraries based on your interests, guiding you between stops. By taking care of the details, the only thing you have to do is get lost in the moment.

Social Profiles

Showcase all the amazing places you have visited to friends and fans and build up a name for yourself as a trusted traveller who can work their way around the globe.

Find Inspiration

You might be stuck in the office, but satisfy that travel itch by browsing through photos to help plan your next trip, find inspiration and begin building your bucket list.

How does it work?

KOMPAS will impact the way people travel by moving away from the existing one-dimensional digital guides, towards a fluid, handpicked collection of places that adapts to your personality over time. All of this is neatly packaged into a simple, easy-to-use application that will save you time and effort, offering that reliable guiding hand when you are out exploring the unknown.
